# Retail_Sales-Dataset
Power BI dashboard project analyzing $2.07M+ retail sales data. Features KPI monitoring, category/store/staff performance analysis, geographic sales mapping, time-series trends, and interactive slicers. Developed using Power BI, Power Query, DAX, and data modeling techniques.

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objectives of this project were to:

- Track overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing stores and product categories
- Evaluate staff contribution to revenue generation
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns
- Monitor sales trends over time
- Support management with actionable business insights

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales Performance Analysis

- Category-wise sales comparison
- Highest and lowest revenue-generating product categories
- Revenue contribution analysis across bicycle segments

### 2. Store Performance Tracking

- Store-wise sales comparison
- Identification of top-performing and underperforming stores
- Revenue contribution by store location

### 3. Staff Performance Evaluation

- Individual staff sales analysis
- Employee ranking based on sales performance
- Productivity tracking for performance evaluation

### 4. Geographic Sales Analysis

- State-wise sales visualization using map charts
- Regional sales distribution analysis
- High-demand region identification

### 5. Time-Series Sales Trends

- Daily sales trend monitoring
- Seasonal demand pattern analysis
- Peak sales period identification

### 6. Interactive Filtering System

Users can dynamically filter dashboard data by:

- State
- Store Name
- Category
- Date Range
- Month

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### Product Category Insights

- Road Bikes generated the highest revenue contribution.
- Mountain Bikes ranked second in overall sales performance.
- Children's Bicycle category generated the lowest revenue contribution, highlighting potential growth opportunities.

### Staff Performance Insights

- Venita emerged as the top-performing sales representative.
- Marcelene maintained strong and consistent sales performance.
- Performance differences highlighted opportunities for targeted training and coaching.

### Store Performance Insights

- Baldwin Bikes contributed the largest share of total revenue.
- Significant sales variation was observed across store locations.

### Sales Trend Insights

- Multiple sales spikes indicated seasonal purchasing behavior.
- Daily sales fluctuations revealed opportunities for inventory and demand planning optimization.
